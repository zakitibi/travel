Transport
Suggested flow & options
Arrivals & departures
Lisbon in · Porto out (or reverse)
Open‑jaw flights save one back‑track and align naturally with the Lisbon → Porto route.
Intercity trains
Lisbon ⇄ Porto · Porto ⇄ Douro
Fast trains connect Lisbon and Porto comfortably; regional lines trace the Douro river with scenic views from the window.
Local mobility
Urban transport & day trips
In cities, rely on metro, trams, and taxis; for Sintra and some Douro viewpoints, private transfers or day‑tour shuttles keep logistics seamless.

Keep one digital wallet with all tickets, seat reservations, and pick‑up times to avoid hunting through emails while on the move.

Accommodation
Area & style suggestions
Lisbon · 3 nights
Chiado / Baixa · Boutique or design hotel
Central neighborhoods keep tram lines, riverfront walks, and hilltop viewpoints within a 10–15 minute walk.
Douro Valley · 2 nights
Vineyard guesthouse (quinta)
Choose a property with on‑site tastings and sunset terraces to reduce daily transfers and maximize downtime.
Porto · 1–2 nights
Ribeira / central Porto
A stay near the river or in the historic grid offers easy access to Port lodges, bridge views, and restaurants.

When booking, prioritize walkability, late check‑out options, and 24‑hour reception for smoother arrivals and departures.
